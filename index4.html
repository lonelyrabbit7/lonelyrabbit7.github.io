Food poverty: global and regional

global trends in food poverty:

The global share of people in food poverty has decreased overall since 1990,and year-on-year since the start
of 1999 , falling from 22% to 6% in 2015.

Regional trends in food poverty:

Since 1990, the number of people living in food poverty has increased by almost 20% in Middle East and North
North Africa, and over 25% in sub-Saharan Africa. These regions now represent 62% of all people in food poverty
